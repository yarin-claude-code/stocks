## Differentiators vs Finviz/Stock Rover

- Single composite score (0–100), fully explained — competitors show raw data
- Domain-based org (AI, Sports) vs GICS sectors — more intuitive for retail
- "Best Overall Investment Today" — competitors overwhelm with data
- Mathematical breakdown per stock — competitors are black-box
